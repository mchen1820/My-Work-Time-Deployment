What is My Work Time?

My Work Time is an AI-powered website providing you with all the tools you may need for your work, whether it's summarizing a document for your job or transcribing a lecture video for school.


Find the Perfect Tool:

Summarization

Reading a lengthy text document, informational video, or code file? Use our summarizer tools to quickly understand the main points!

Transcription

Listening to a long video or audio file that you'd rather read or parse through as text? Transcribe it with our transcription tools!

Quiz Generation

Studying a long document or video for an upcoming assignment or test? Test your knowledge with a sample quiz from our quiz generator!

Image-Text OCR

Working with images that contain writing or letters you wish you could copy? Extract text from images with our OCR tools!

Coding Help

Struggling to write some code? Can't understand what your code is doing? Get help with these situations and more with our Code Helper!
